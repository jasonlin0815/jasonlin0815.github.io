Fragments

After an impulsive purchase of HTC Vive system, and of course couple days of fun VR experience. I decided to play around Unity3D to create a VR world with integrated APIs. This just a small demonstration on what I created: locomotion control that uses 3rd party open-source, interaction in the world through controller (it calls haptic pulse). Hoping this, in future, will expand and continue to a bigger project.
