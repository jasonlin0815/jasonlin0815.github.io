Pinball Arcade

This was the final project of ECE362 in Purdue University; the goal was to demonstrate knowledge in programming microcontroller unit. We decided to create a primitive arcade with two play mode: score, timing. The idea is from my childhood; every weekend night, my parents will take me to a Taiwanese night market, where we will walk on a street filled with foods and mini games. This was my favorite game (actually the only one I was able to win prizes from).
